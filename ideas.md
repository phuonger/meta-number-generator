# Meta Number Generator — Design Brainstorm

## Selected Approach: "Meta Presentation Style" — Corporate Gradient Expressionism

**Design Movement:** Official Meta brand presentation aesthetic — the bright, multicolour gradient style used in Meta's internal and external slide decks.

**Core Principles:**
- Bright multicolour gradient background (blue→teal→green with warm pink/orange top-left)
- Large bold white rounded typography (Helvetica Neue / Arial)
- Clean white frosted card for the number display (glassmorphism)
- Official Meta infinity wordmark in bottom-right corner

**Color Philosophy:** The gradient uses Meta's full brand spectrum — from warm peach/coral in the top-left corner through Meta blue in the center to teal/green in the bottom-right. This creates an energetic, optimistic feel that matches Meta's brand identity. White is the primary foreground color for maximum contrast and readability.

**Layout Paradigm:** Centered hero layout with the generated number as the absolute focal point. The number card floats in the center of the viewport with controls below. Expandable panels for range settings and history slide in below the main card.

**Signature Elements:**
- Number scramble/decode animation (random digits → final number)
- Pulse rings emanating from the card on generation
- Confetti dots floating upward on each generation
- Frosted glass cards with subtle white borders

**Interaction Philosophy:** Each generation feels satisfying — the number scrambles through random digits before revealing the final result. Pulse rings and confetti provide visual celebration. Controls are minimal and tucked away until needed.

**Animation:** Spring-based number scaling, scramble decode effect, pulse ring expansion, confetti float-up, smooth panel slide transitions.

**Typography System:** "Helvetica Neue" / Arial for all text — matching Meta's corporate typography. Extra bold (900) for the display number, bold (800) for headings, semibold (600) for controls.
